# Calorie-burnt-prediction
Introducing our Exercise Calorie Estimation App! This intuitive tool harnesses advanced machine learning algorithms to accurately track and estimate calories burned during various workouts. Users can input age, gender, height, weight, exercise duration, and body temperature for personalized calorie predictions. Additionally, the app provides real-time feedback and suggestions for optimizing workouts based on individual metrics. Developed with Python, Flask, HTML, and CSS, our app offers seamless integration and a user-friendly interface. Take charge of your fitness journey today and reach your health goals with our innovative Exercise Calorie Estimation App!
